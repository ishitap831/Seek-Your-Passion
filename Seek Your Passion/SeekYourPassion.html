Our Purpose

According to Pennsylvania State University, 25 to 50 percent of students enter college with an undecided major, and 75% of students change their majors at least once before graduating. Not only that, but a Gallup survey showed that 71% of workers aren't fully "engaged" in their jobs.
Seek Your Passion was created to combat this problem. We encourage people to explore their interests and find what they are really passionate about. This way, people can have more time to delve deeper into what they love to do, and increase their overall quality of life.

Ready to get started? Simply go to our "What's Your Interest?" tab and click on whatever subject interests you. Clicking on a subject leads you to a list of programs, classes, and opportunities that you can use to get started on seeking you passion. Good luck!
