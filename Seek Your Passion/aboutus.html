About Us



Seek Your Passion was founded on July 11, 2016 by five girls: Ishita Patel, Lily Do, Hamna Khan, Jamie Chavez, and Getzy Correa. As they participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, they wanted to make their final project meaningful and helpful to the community. Thus, Seek Your Passion was created. As seniors and juniors in high school, they acknowledged the sad reality that many adults in the workforce aren't passionate about their jobs. By creating Seek Your Passion, they gave pre-teens and young adults the chance to expand their horizons and find a career they look forward to in the future.
